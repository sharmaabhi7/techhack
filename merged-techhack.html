About Rai University

UGC-Recognized NAAC-Accredited

Rai University, Ahmedabad (UGC-recognized & NAAC-accredited), established under Gujarat Act No. 12 of 2012, is dedicated to providing quality, skill-oriented, and holistic education across disciplines like Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Law, Agriculture, and Sciences.

Spread over a lush green campus, the university blends curriculum with life skills to shape globally competent and ethical leaders. With a strong focus on research, industry collaborations, and international tie-ups (e.g., University of Pardubice, Czech Republic – Erasmus+ program), Rai University ensures students gain practical exposure, employable skills, and an entrepreneurial mindset.

Its holistic approach nurtures both curricular and extracurricular growth, leadership, teamwork, and hidden talents, while robust mentoring supports students and alumni alike. Scholarships for meritorious and underprivileged students further strengthen Rai University's commitment to inclusive education and societal values.

About Rai School of Engineering

Established 2013

Rai School of Engineering is a constituent School of Rai University established in 2013 consists of M.Tech, MCA, B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc IT and Diploma Programmes. RSE emphasizes on Skill Development, Project Based Learning and ICT enabled pedagogies to provide Outcome Based Education to the budding Student fraternity.

RSE is powered by distinguished Faculties who focus on Quality Research Papers, Chapters and Books in addition to Patent publication and grant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Some frequently asked questions for you.

What are the prizes?

Winners get up to ₹20,000* + Cash Prizes and Goodies

Prize Distribution:
• Cash prizes for top performing teams
• Exciting goodies and swag for participants
• Certificates for all participants
• Special recognition for innovative solutions

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open to all students enrolled in colleges/universities across Gujarat, India. Both individual participants and teams of up to 4 members are welcome.

What should I bring?

Participants should bring their laptops, chargers, and any other hardware/software tools they need. Food and refreshments will be provided during the event.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, the registration fees are as follows:
- ₹500 per team for students from other institutions
- ₹300 per team for Rai University students
Registration fee includes lunch, refreshments, and participation kit.
